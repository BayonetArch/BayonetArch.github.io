Egg fried rice

Description:

Egg fried rice is a classic dish featuring stir-fried rice combined with scrambled eggs, vegetables, and seasonings. Itâ€™s a simple yet flavorful meal that highlights the savory richness of soy sauce and the crunch of fresh vegetables or add-ins like peas and carrots. The eggs add a creamy, velvety texture that balances the firmness of the rice, creating a satisfying bite.

This versatile dish is a great way to repurpose leftover cooked rice, turning it into a hearty meal or side dish. The combination of umami from soy sauce or oyster sauce, the slight sweetness of vegetables, and the savory eggs makes it a well-rounded and delicious meal. You can customize it by adding protein like chicken or shrimp or extra vegetables for added nutrition and variety.

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 cups leftover cooked rice
  • 1-2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1-2 eggs
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish
Steps:
  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes to dry out
  2. Heat 1/2 tablespoon of oil in a pan, add the beaten egg, and scramble it. Set aside
  3. Heat the remaining oil in the same pan, then add the diced onion and minced garlic
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps
  6. Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using). Mix well
  7. Add the scrambled egg to the rice mixture and stir-fry everything together
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  9. Transfer to a serving plate, garnish with green onions, and serve immediately
